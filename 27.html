Ethereum Blockchain as a Service (EthBaaS) on MS Azure

Essentially the idea is to allow people to develop DApps on the cloud - 'incubating disruption'. I think this is a good example of how existing players are trying to jump on the train - we will see more of this. Leveraging existing ecosystems with the distributed paradigm is a promising avenue, imo.
