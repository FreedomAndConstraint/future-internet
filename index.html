Future Internet technologies

Future Internet technologies

This gitbook documents a study of future Internet technologies. The document has emerged from notes of an internal mini-workshop at the Global Brain Institute at the end of 2015 by @spaceweaver and @vveitas. 2015 was a year when it became apparent that a broad family of new technologies is emerging with serious potential to disrupt the whole Internet ecosystem and beyond - with repercussions to legal, economic and social governance systems 'in the real'.

We call this family of technologies distributed IT governance systems. The technology space within it is very diverse is dynamic: new systems / programs / approaches come and go almost every day. It is rather difficult to understand what is going on with the Internet currently. While specific technologies are informative and shape the direction of the development, they also carry certain more general and basic ideas behind them. Therefore, we decided to take a look at the emerging technology space of distributed IT governance systems from the conceptual perspective. We feel that this perspective is often missing from the usually technical discourse (sometimes supplemented by a certain amount of hype - substantiated or not).

Considering the dynamics of the technology space and still high amount of uncertainty (which, actually, may never reduce), the following text does not represent a static document. We hope to update and improve it as new technologies, ideas and understanding come. The process can be greatly enhanced by the open discussion. Please add your comments / suggest topics / technologies using Hypothes.is annotation system.
